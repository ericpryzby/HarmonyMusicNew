Acoustics, ergonomic features, and aesthetics recall the spirit of this famous model. The Reference 54 offers a rich tone color, very centered tuning balance, precise keywork, comfortable handling, and a specific finish with a fine engraving and dark lacquer. This classic stands out with its great ease of playing, flexibility and power. Achieve new levels of maximum musical expressiveness with the Reference 54.

Selmer Paris now applies the “Reference” concept to the alto saxophone. A strong personality for an instrument of modern design and making, in which each musician will find the legendary Mark VI feeling. The Selmer Reference 54, where classic history meets modern design.

  • Type: Alto Saxophone
  • Make: Selmer Paris
  • Model: Reference 54
  • Body Material: Brass - Brushed Finish
  • Body Construction: Ribbed Body Construction
  • Made In: France
  • Case: Contoured Case
